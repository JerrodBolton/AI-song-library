# 🎧 Jerrod's AI Song Portfolio

A futuristic, space-themed music portfolio showcasing AI-generated songs created throughout my college journey.

This project combines **music, AI, and web development** into a single interactive experience where users can explore, listen, and reflect on different tracks tied to personal growth, academic work, and creative exploration.

---

## 🌌 **Theme & Inspiration**

This website is inspired by a **cosmic environment**:
- 🌑 Deep space background
- 🌅 Glowing orange horizon (sun)
- 🟢 Neon green nebula atmosphere
- 🪨 Dark, grounded foreground

The design reflects:
> "Standing on another planet, looking out at a new world while exploring music."

---

## 🎵 **Features**

- 🎧 Play AI-generated songs directly in the browser  
- 🗂️ Organized music categories:
  - **School** 🎓
  - **Personal** ❤️
  - **Concept** 💡  
- 🎨 Color-coded categories for easy navigation  
- 🌙 Clean, dark-mode UI  
- ⚡ Smooth, non-distracting user experience  
- 📱 Responsive design (mobile + desktop)

---

## 🚫 **Design Rules**

- ❌ No bouncing or distracting animations  
- ❌ No autoplay audio  
- ✅ Smooth and stable interactions  
- ✅ Focus on clarity, atmosphere, and usability
